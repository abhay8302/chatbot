# STUV.ai Features

STUV.ai offers 13 powerful AI features:

1. **AI Image Generation** - Generate studio-quality product images
2. **Bulk Generation** - Process multiple images at once
3. **AI Video Generation** - Create product videos from images
4. **AI Image Magic Suite** - Advanced image editing tools
5. **AI Upscaler** - Enhance image resolution
6. **Object Replace** - Replace objects in images
7. **Fabric Match** - Match fabric textures
8. **Virtual Try-On** - Virtual fitting for products
9. **See In Your Room** - Visualize products in room settings
10. **AI Product Detailing** - Enhance product details
11. **Shopify Push** - Direct integration with Shopify
12. **AI Website Builder** - Generate product websites
13. **Background Magic** - Intelligent background replacement
