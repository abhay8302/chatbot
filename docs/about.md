# About STUV.ai

STUV.ai is an AI platform for furniture businesses that generates studio, catalog, lifestyle, and editorial visuals from a single product image in under 60 seconds.

## Problem Solved
Traditional product photography is slow (days/weeks), expensive ($500–$5,000/shoot). Stuv AI generates images in under 60 seconds per product for ₹15–₹300 per image.

## Platform Statistics
- 10M+ images generated
- 1M+ videos created
- 50,000+ registered users
- 99.2% accuracy
